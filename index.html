SECRET LEVEL UNLOCKED!

Congratulations, Legend! Choosing the Thick & Gooey path reveals ultimate potential. You can STUFF these behemoths!

How? Flatten a dough ball slightly, make a dent, add ~1 tsp of Nutella or Pistachio Spread, wrap the dough around it, seal, and bake as directed (maybe add 1-2 min).
